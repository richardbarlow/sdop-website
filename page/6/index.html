Lots of Things!

Posted on April 15, 2012 by Berry

Hello intrepid watchers of this space. It has been a busy 3 weeks since our last blog update, let me tell you…

Firstly the ever helpful Joyce from ECS had 1000 very professional A5 leaflets printed for us, to be distributed as we see fit.

Then Matt Lokes put in a sterling effort and made us a team’s worth of awesome t-shirts, with a promise of more to come.
