It’s all starting to come together!

Posted on March 19, 2012 by Alex

It’s been a big week! We’ve booked our flights back from Ulaanbaatar and we’ll be flying through Beijing! We’re all pretty excited about this as we’ll get to experience a bit of Chinese culture in the 18 hours between our flights! Although we’re not sure if we can leave the airport when we’re there it’ll still be awesome!
